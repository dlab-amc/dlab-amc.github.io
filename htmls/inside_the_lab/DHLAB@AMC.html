MISSION & VISION

Our mission is to harness the collective power of knowledge and cutting-edge technology across diverse domains to deliver innovative solutions that promote human well-being, with a particular emphasis on advancing healthcare practices that contribute to a healthier and happier existence. In parallel, we are dedicated to nurturing a cadre of imaginative and resilient professionals capable of perpetuating this quest.

HISTORY

D.Lab. opened in March 2022 at Asan Medical Center, University of Ulsan College of Medicine. D.Lab.'s predecessor was Healthcare Solution Lab., which was originally established at the College of Engineering Science at Chonnam National University. It was later renewed as D.Lab. when Professor Hangsik Shin, the Principal Investigator (PI), relocated to Asan Medical Center, University of Ulsan College of Medicine.
